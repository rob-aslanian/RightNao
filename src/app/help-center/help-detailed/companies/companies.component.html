Companies & Organizations

How to Register a Company/Organization on HyperCube?

▴ ▾

A company helps members learn more about your business products and services, and job opportunities.

Before creating an account for your company, you’ll need to create a HyperCube account with your real first and last name.

To create a Company or Organization:

  1. Click the Profile picture icon in the top right corner of your homepage
  2. Select Company or Organization
  3. Enter details of your company or organization in Registration Form
    HyperCube needs the following information:
    • Name
    • Industry
    • Company/organization type
    • Email
    • Phone
    • Address – Street address, Building number, City, Country
    • Vat number is optional
    • Public URL will be automatically generated
  4. Check the verification box to confirm you have the right to act on behalf of that company or Organization in the creation of the page
  5. Click icon to register.
  6. You'll be prompted to verify your email address

After creating Company/organization, you can enter further details to build your brand by going to Company Profile. Learn more about adding and editing Profile information.

Company/Organization profile Overview

▴ ▾

You can add/edit/delete your company information such as:

  • About us
  • Founders
  • Milestones
  • Awards
  • Products
  • Services
  • Location

At any time by clicking into the appropriate text box and making your changes.

You can upload a cover image (top of the page) and a profile image below that exemplifies your company and culture.

To add an image for the first time, click Upload image from computer to select a file, drag to reposition image and save it.

Logo image - RECOMMENDED IMAGE SIZE 180 (w) x 180 (h) pixels

Cover image - RECOMMENDED IMAGE SIZE 1920 (w) x 240 (h) pixels

Your cover image may have to be adjusted to fit the screen which may involve trimming the image horizontally or vertically. We recommend uploading a high-quality image with as little text as possible to ensure an optimal display on all devices and screen sizes. Our accepted image formats for uploads are PNG or JPEG.

How to add/edit/delete about us section to Company/Organization Profile?

▴ ▾

To add about us section to your profile click on icon

Enter a description in the description text box (up to 1000 characters) that provides an overview of your company.

Enter a Mission (up to 500 characters) of your company/organization.

You can edit your company/Organization information such as Sub-industry, Type, Date founded, size and Parking at any time by clicking into the appropriate text box

How to add/edit/delete Founder to Company/Organization Profile?

▴ ▾

To add founder to your profile, click on the Plus icon beside the Founders section.

Enter full name of the founder and title of position.

If user with this name is already registered on Hypercube, you will see the list from where you can select the founder. Enter the title of position and click save.

You can still add the founder manually if he/she is not registered on hypercube. You will need to add his/her picture, enter title of position and click save.

To delete a founder, click on pencil icon and click delete button.

How to add/edit/delete Milestone to Company/Organization Profile?

▴ ▾

Milestones help to tell the story of your business.

Follow these steps to add the milestone:

  1. Find the milestone section on company/Organization Profile and click on the Plus icon.
  2. Upload a photo. Recommended image size for a milestone is 549 pixels wide by 309 pixels long.
  3. Add the date.
  4. Add the title.
  5. Add the Description (150 characters) – this is optional but Adding a story makes the milestone much more interesting.
  6. Click save.
  7. You have also option to save and add another.

How to add/edit/delete location to Company/Organization Profile?

▴ ▾

To add location section to your company profile

  1. Find the Location section on your Company/Organization Profile and click on the Plus icon.
  2. Give name to location. For example: Dublin branch, Zara in Clarendon Centre.
  3. Fill up address form. Note: you can set this address as the main office.
  4. You can add Business Hours for this location and Phone Number.
  5. Click save

Reviews - Overview

▴ ▾

To write a review on Hypercube:

  1. Go to Company/Organization Profile
  2. Click on More button and select write a review.
  3. On pop up window click on stars to rate.
  4. The Headline field is required for submission.
  5. Add review description (optional) up to 500 character.
  6. Click Post.

Note: you can submit only one review per company.

To edit or delete submitted Review

  1. Go to your Profile
  2. Select Reviews Tab
  3. Find the review you want to edit or delete
  4. Click on icon
  5. Make changes and save.
  6. Or click delete button, if you want to remove it.

How to add or Remove Admins on Your Company Page?

▴ ▾

To add an admin to your Page:

  1. Go to Company/Organization account settings.
  2. Select Manage admins.
  3. To add a new admin : Select the type of admin you want to add.
  4. Type the name of the member you’d like to add in the Add new admin by name… text field.
    Note: You must be connected to the requesting member to grant admin access.
  5. Enter your password and click confirm

To remove an admin from your Page:

  1. Go to Company/Organization account settings.
  2. Select Manage admins.
  3. Locate the admin’s name, click on delete icon on user’s card you want to remove.
  4. Enter your password and click confirm.

How to Deactivate a Company or Organization account?

▴ ▾

Your Hypercube Company or Organization Page can only be removed by designated admins of the page or the Hypercube customer support team.

A Company/Organization must have fewer than 50 affiliated employees in order for us to remove the page.

Unlike your personal account, a Company Page affects all employees and not a single person or entity. If your company is no longer in operation or if it has been rebranded, please keep in mind that it does not mean you should remove the page from Hypercube.

You can deactivate your Company/Organization Page through your Account / Admin Tools if it meets all the following guidelines:

  1. You are an admin of the Company/Organization
  2. The Company Page has fewer than 50 affiliated employees.
  3. The Company Page has no active ad campaigns.

To deactivate your Company or Organization:

  1. Go to Company/organization account settings.
  2. Find General settings >>> Additional settings and click Deactivate account.
  3. In the pop-up window, click enter Password and then click confirm.
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email that your Company/Organization was successfully deactivated.
    Note: If the Company Page couldn't be deactivated, you'll receive an error message.
